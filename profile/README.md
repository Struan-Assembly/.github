## What We Do

Great operations don't run on good intentions. They run on systems that are thoughtfully designed, deeply integrated, and built to hold up under pressure. That's what we make.

Our portfolio spans digital signage, guest-facing experiences, kitchen operations, point-of-sale integration, analytics, loyalty, and more -- a growing suite of platforms built around the real workflows of the industries we serve. Two examples:

**[Stagehand](https://www.struanassembly.com/stagehand)** -- Enterprise digital signage built for multi-location operations. Content is managed centrally, but every location runs independently. When the network drops, the show goes on.

**[Bridge](https://www.struanassembly.com/bridge)** -- The connective tissue between your operational systems. Bridge integrates point-of-sale, kitchen display, analytics, and more into a unified data layer -- so your stack finally works as one.

[See everything we build at struanassembly.com](https://www.struanassembly.com)
